About Me

Hello! I'm a Friendly Neighborhood Person that likes creating things. In everything I make I like giving it some sort of untold story. I like making character designs and drawing creatures. I want to share glimpses into their worlds were they face challenges much like our own. I value making things fun and entertaining, even when I'm delving into serious topics. I know this talk of me valuing fun makes me seem unprofessional I just think it's such an important element in any creative endevor. Right now I'm pursuing a degree in Graphic Design but I am also an aspiring Game Developer.
